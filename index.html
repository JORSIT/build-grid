How to Contribute?

Step 1: Fork/Clone this repository

Step 2: Find your card and update it to "implemented" status

Step 3: Add your app section to the HTML and implement your assigned functionality

Step 4: Create a new branch and submit a Pull Request to be reviewd and merged

Step 5: See your app go live for everyone to enjoy!
